Our Bio

The Valleymen is an indie rock band from Denver, Colorado, known for their vibey, energetic sound and lush harmonies. The band's members, Bryan Tadlock, Bennett Miner, Maanasi Gowda, and Rider Silvey, draw inspiration from artists like Hippocampus, Flipturn, and Mt Joy, creating a unique blend of modern indie vibes and classic rock/folk elements. Their songs capture the essence of indie rock with an airy, feel-good atmosphere, leaving a lasting impression on their listeners.
